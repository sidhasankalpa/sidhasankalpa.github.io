Sidha Sankalpa Moharana

Ph.D. Candidate in Atmosphere and Ocean Sciences at IIT Bhubaneswar

Welcome to my personal webpage! I am a passionate researcher (CSIR-SRF) focusing on the current changes in global tropical cyclone activity. My work involves analyzing the historical as well as future trends of tropical cyclone activity and associated drivers. I am always eager to explore new ideas and collaborate on exciting projects.

View CV

Research Interests

My research lies at the intersection of Oceanography, Atmospheric Science, and Space Weather. I am particularly interested in the following areas:

Tropical Cyclones

Recent changes in the tropical cyclone activity in the global oceans due to climate change.

Troposphere-Ionosphere Interactions

Coupling of the Ionosphere with the Troposphere via gravity waves.

Transient Luminous Events

Very short-lived electrical phenomena in the upper atmosphere, also known as upper atmospheric lightning.
